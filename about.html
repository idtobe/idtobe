Welcome to my Portfolio

I am a CG Artist with a passion for creating stunning 3D visuals and using new technologies. I have experience in modelling, texturing, lighting, rendering and animation using various software such as Blender, Unreal Engine and 3dsMax.
My background as an industrial designer allowed me to work very early with CAD and 3D, while providing me with a strong user-centred approach.

I am currently developing new skills within programming, focusing mainly on XR, including AR/VR for web.
In this portfolio, you will find some of my work that showcases my skills and style. I hope you enjoy browsing through my portfolio and feel free to contact me if you have any questions or feedback.
Thank you for your time and interest!
